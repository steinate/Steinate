## 👨‍💻 About Me

> Third-year Ph.D. candidate in Electronic Engineering at **Tsinghua University**, jointly trained at **Shanghai AI Laboratory** (InternRobotics Group).
>
> B.S. in Electronic Engineering from **Shanghai Jiao Tong University** (2023).

My research sits at the intersection of **3D Vision** and **Embodied AI**, building integrated systems that unify perception, planning, and control for autonomous robots.

> 🎯 **Ultimate Vision**: Pioneering the realization of **Artificial Fluctlight** — a theoretical framework for human-like conscious embodiment in synthetic environments.
